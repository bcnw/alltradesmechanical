ABOUT US



Founded in September 1992, All Trades Mechanical Inc. is a leader in the mechanical contracting industry. Our team is comprised of service and installation experts, with the ability and flexibility to construct multiple projects of different scopes and in various locations. We work throughout the United States, with security clearance for access to government bases.



Our customer base includes:
School districts, hospitals, federal government facilities, commercial office buildings, restaurants and bars, bank buildings, yacht clubs, county jails, correction facilities, catering facilities, bowling alleys, food banks and commercial fishing boats.



Service areas include, but are not limited to:
Kitsap, Mason, Pierce & King Counties


Our installation team does work in these areas, as well as nationally.
